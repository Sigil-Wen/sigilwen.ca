On Hiring

Notes on Hiring

  • A hires As, B hires Cs. Never compromise or you face harder decision down the line
  • Read: Finding the Next Steve Jobs
  • You never regret firing someone. Only that you didn't fire faster
  • Whenever possible, prune. Fire fast. Lean teams go a long way
  • Culture is recoverable. It requires removing the misaligned people and reinvigorating the team
  • It's important for hires to not burn out the founders - early stage that is existential to the survival of the company
  • Found if you have high enough conviction to recruit the best talent. Otherwise join the rocketship with the best talent
  • To find talent, do not be afraid to text everyone smart in your network. I would message hundreds of smart people if they knew engineers
  • Pairing INCENTIVEs with hiring asks lets you tap into your 2nd and even 3rd degree networks. "I will have Naval grant you advisor stock"
  • It should be obvious why everyone is integral to the company. Everyone should know this. Especially if you are <15 headcount
  • Each new hire should be the best at something.
  • Great talent shines pretty much immediately
  • Ex-founders make great Barrels
